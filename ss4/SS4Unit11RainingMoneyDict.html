Raining Money

  1. People were driving along the highway when they noticed money flying in the air and blowing around all over the highway.
  2. They stopped their cars and got out and starting chasing money—putting it into their shirts, bags, and pockets.
  3. Apparently, an armored vehicle was transferring money when one of it’s doors came open.
  4. In all, $175,000 had been spilled onto the highway.
  5. The police said that any one who kept the money was committing a crime.
  6. The security company said, “It doesn’t matter whether or not it’s a crime, that money is long gone. We’ll never get it back.”
