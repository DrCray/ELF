Old Men Rock

  1. Two elderly men from a nursing home went missing Saturday night.
  2. The nurses were worried because some of their patients have dementia and get accidentally lost.
  3. The staff reported the missing patients to the police.
  4. Officer Caldwell knew that the only thing going on that late at night was a heavy metal rock concert.
  5. So the officer went to the concert to look for the old men.
  6. Officer Caldwell found the elderly men at the first aid tent.
  7. They were sitting in front of a large outdoor fan and sipping on fruit juice.
  8. Bud and Bob plan on going to the concert next year.
  9. Next time we’ll plan it out and notify everyone.
  10. We don’t want to worry anyone again, said Bud.
