Friends TV show

Title
Article




"Friends" is centered around a group of six young friends who live in New York City.
They are all single at the beginning of the show.
The girls are Monica, Rachel, and Phoebe.
The guys are Chandler, Joey, and Ross.


Monica and Ross are siblings (brother and sister);




Monica and Rachel are former high-school classmates and become roommates after the first episode;




Chandler and Joey are roommates;




Phoebe is Monica's former roommate.



In later episodes, we will find out everyone's jobs.



Ross is a museum curator and university professor.




Joey is an aspiring actor.




Chandler is a data processing specialist, which means he has a steady office job.




Rachel has never had a job because she lives off her father's money.
However, she decides to be more independent and takes a job at the coffee shop.




Monica is a chef,




Phoebe is a masseuse (she gives massages).




Personalities


Ross is a bit of a nerd, but he is very educated.
His wife left him for her lesbian lover.
Later, we will see that she was pregnant with his child when she left him.




Joey tends to be a little dense sometimes. He's not stupid, he just doesn't spend much time thinking about things.
In other words, he's not a deep thinker.
Joey dates a lot, but doesn't want a serious relationship.




Chandler is good at his job and he is a jokester (likes to say funny things).




Monica has a strong personality and often acts like the leader of the group.
Monica likes for everything to be organized and put in the right place.
She always keeps her apartment clean and in order.




Rachel is the pretty girl. She is naive about money and is a bit self-centered.




Phoebe is, well.... Phoebe.
She sees the world in a different way than other people, this makes her a bit strange.
Nevertheless, she's cheerful, friendly, and lovable.
She's also not afraid to be herself.
She won't change her thinking just to please others.
