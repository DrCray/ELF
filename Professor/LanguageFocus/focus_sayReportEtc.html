Language Focus: Reporting Verbs in News Stories

These verbs replace "say" to show how certain or reliable a statement is — from rumor to confirmed fact. Use them to make your story retellings sound natural and precise.

1. Rumor / Very Uncertain

allege, claim, suggest

Nuance: Signals doubt, lack of proof, or unconfirmed info

  • The suspect alleges police mistreated him during questioning.
  • Witnesses claim they saw a bright light in the sky.
  • Early reports suggest the fire may have been started deliberately.
2. Strong Belief / Possible Doubt

insist, maintain

Nuance: Speaker strongly believes it (but others may disagree)

  • The driver insists the other car ran the red light.
  • The company maintains that all safety checks were completed.
3. Neutral Reporting

say, report, state, add, note

Nuance: Just reporting information without strong judgment

  • Police say the incident happened around 2 a.m.
  • Witnesses report hearing a loud explosion.
  • Officials state that no one was seriously injured.
  • The officer added that the investigation is ongoing.
4. Explanation / Description

explain, describe

Nuance: Focuses on giving reasons or vivid details

  • The survivor explained that thick smoke made escape difficult.
  • Witnesses described the animal as large and dark with glowing eyes.
5. Admission / Confirmation

admit, confirm

Nuance: Reluctant truth or official verification

  • The man admitted breaking into the house after hours of questioning.
  • Authorities confirmed that three people were taken to hospital.
6. Official / Proven Fact

announce, reveal

Nuance: Formal public statement or surprising new information

  • The mayor announced new safety measures following the incident.
  • Investigators revealed video evidence showing the suspect nearby.

Quick Summary & Tip

Use these verbs to show reliability: claim/suggest for unproven info, say/report for neutral facts, admit/confirm for accepted truth, and announce/reveal for big news. Avoid repeating "say" — try 3–4 different verbs when retelling a story!
