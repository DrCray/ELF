The Golden Pairs

Words that stick together like glue.

In English, some words are best friends. They always appear in the same order. Swap them, and you’ll sound like a robot!

The Pride

Pride and Joy

Meaning: Someone or something that makes you very proud.
"That vintage motorcycle is his pride and joy."

The Hospitality

Wine and Dine UK

Meaning: To entertain someone with expensive food and drink.
"The company wined and dined the clients to close the deal."

The State

Alive and Well

Meaning: Still existing, active, or successful.
"The tradition of handmade pottery is alive and well in this village."

The Distance

Length and Breadth

Meaning: Everywhere in a specific place.
"They traveled the length and breadth of the country."

The Search

High and Low

Meaning: Looking everywhere (similar to far and wide).
"I’ve searched high and low for my passport, but I can't find it."

The Result

Null and Void

Meaning: Having no legal force; invalid.
"If you don't sign the document, the contract is null and void."

The Order

Rules and Regulations

Meaning: Official instructions on what can and cannot be done.
"Life is full of rules and regulations that's the very definition of 'civilization."

The Nature

Facts and Figures

Meaning: Precise and detailed information/statistics.
"Show me the facts and figures that support your argument."

The Comparison

Give or Take

Meaning: Approximately (e.g., plus or minus).
"It will take four hours, give or take a few minutes."

The Progress

Onwards and Upwards

Meaning: Continuing to improve or be successful.
"The team had a tough year, but it's onwards and upwards from here."
