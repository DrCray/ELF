The Golden Pairs

Words that stick together like glue.

In English, some words are best friends. They always appear in the same order. Swap them, and you’ll sound like a robot!

The Vibe

Short and Sweet

Meaning: Brief in a pleasant way.
"I'll keep this speech short and sweet."

The Sound

Loud and Clear

Meaning: Very easy to hear and understand.
"I hear you loud and clear."

The Cleanliness

Spick and Span UK

Meaning: Extremely clean and tidy.
"The house was spick and span before the guests arrived."

The Comparison

More or Less

Meaning: Approximately; almost.
"The job is more or less finished."

The Distance

Near and Far

Meaning: In many different places.
"People came from near and far to see the show."

The Lifestyle

Part and Parcel

Meaning: An essential or unavoidable part of something.
"Stress is part and parcel of this job."

The Logic

Cause and Effect

Meaning: The relationship between actions and results.
"We are studying the cause and effect of global warming."

The Survival

Sink or Swim

Meaning: To fail or succeed by your own efforts without help.
"In this industry, it's sink or swim."

The Effort

Blood, Sweat, and Tears

Meaning: Extremely hard work and sacrifice (a rare "trinomial").
"I put my blood, sweat, and tears into this project."

The Relationship

Through Thick and Thin

Meaning: Staying together through good times and bad times.
"They have been friends through thick and thin."
