The Golden Pairs

Words that stick together like glue.

In English, some words are best friends. They always appear in the same order. Swap them, and you’ll sound like a robot!

The Hierarchy

Rank and File UK

Meaning: The ordinary members of an organization, not the leaders.
"The rank and file members were unhappy with the new contract."

The Speed

Quick and Dirty

Meaning: Done fast and cheaply, without much care for quality.
"It's just a quick and dirty solution until we find a better one."

The Variety

Odds and Ends

Meaning: Various small, unimportant objects or leftovers.
"I've got a few odds and ends to pack before the move."

The Truth

Pure and Simple

Meaning: Used to emphasize that there are no other factors involved.
"It was a case of jealousy, pure and simple."

The Choice

Sooner or Later

Meaning: Inevitably; at some time in the future.
"Sooner or later, you're going to have to face the music."

The Effort

Tooth and Nail

Meaning: To fight with great energy and determination.
"She fought tooth and nail to keep her business open."

The Condition

High and Mighty

Meaning: Behaving as if you are much more important than others.
"He's been acting all high and mighty since his promotion."

The Routine

Wash and Wear

Meaning: Clothing that doesn't need ironing after washing.
"I only buy wash and wear shirts for my business trips."

The Logic

Rhyme or Reason

Meaning: A logical explanation or sense (usually used in the negative).
"There is no rhyme or reason to these new tax laws."

The Scale

Far and Wide

Meaning: Over a very large area.
"The news of the discovery spread far and wide."
