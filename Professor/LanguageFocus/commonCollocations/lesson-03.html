Sharing Information

Lesson 3  ·  General Communication

B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 give information
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆The tourist office is happy to give information about local attractions.General
  • ◆Can you give me some information about the bus schedule?Casual
  • ◆Authorities refused to give information about the ongoing investigation.News
  • ◆The sales team was trained to give accurate information to potential clients.Professional
  • ◆Staff are expected to give information to customers clearly and politely.Workplace
2 provide information
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆The website provides information on how to apply for a visa.General
  • ◆Can you provide some information about what happened?Casual
  • ◆The health ministry agreed to provide information on the outbreak to the public.News
  • ◆All applicants must provide information about their previous employment.Professional
  • ◆The onboarding guide provides information on company procedures and policies.Workplace
3 gather information
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Before making any big decision, it's wise to gather information first.General
  • ◆Let me gather some information and get back to you.Casual
  • ◆Journalists spent weeks gathering information before publishing the investigation.News
  • ◆The research team gathered information from over 500 participants.Professional
  • ◆The safety officer gathered information from staff about the incident.Workplace
4 find information
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆It's easier than ever to find information online these days.General
  • ◆I couldn't find any information about it anywhere.Casual
  • ◆Investigators are working to find information about the suspect's whereabouts.News
  • ◆Analysts were asked to find information on competitor pricing strategies.Professional
  • ◆Use the internal database to find information about past orders.Workplace

share information

  • ◆The two agencies agreed to share information to speed up the investigation.News
