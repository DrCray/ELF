Explaining & Illustrating

Lesson 4  ·  General Communication

B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 give an explanation
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆When you're late, it's polite to give an explanation.General
  • ◆Come on — give me an explanation. What happened?Casual
  • ◆The airline was forced to give an explanation for the three-day delay.News
  • ◆The manager gave a clear explanation of the new reporting structure.Professional
  • ◆The trainer gave an explanation of each safety procedure before the drill.Workplace
2 explain a problem
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Take a deep breath and try to explain the problem calmly.General
  • ◆Just explain the problem to me from the beginning.Casual
  • ◆The engineer was called in to explain the problem that caused the blackout.News
  • ◆The consultant explained the problem in detail before proposing a solution.Professional
  • ◆If something goes wrong, explain the problem to your supervisor right away.Workplace
3 give an example
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆It's always easier to understand a new concept when someone gives an example.General
  • ◆Can you give me an example of what you mean?Casual
  • ◆The professor gave an example of how inflation affects everyday purchasing power.News
  • ◆During the presentation, she gave several examples of successful case studies.Professional
  • ◆The trainer gave an example of the correct way to log a customer complaint.Workplace
4 provide an example
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Good teachers provide examples to make abstract ideas concrete.General
  • ◆Can you provide an example? I'm still not sure I get it.Casual
  • ◆The report provides examples of companies that have successfully reduced emissions.News
  • ◆Applicants should provide examples of past achievements in their cover letter.Professional
  • ◆The manual provides examples of common errors and how to correct them.Workplace

illustrate a point
