B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 complete a task
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆There's a real sense of satisfaction in completing a task you've been putting off.General
  • ◆Just complete the task and then you can relax — it won't take long.Casual
  • ◆Volunteers were asked to complete a series of tasks as part of the community project.News
  • ◆All team members are expected to complete their assigned tasks before the end of the day.Professional
  • ◆Each worker is responsible for completing their tasks before signing off at the end of the shift.Workplace
2 assign a task
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆When working in a group, it helps to assign tasks based on each person's strengths.General
  • ◆Who assigned you that task? That doesn't seem like your usual kind of work.Casual
  • ◆The project coordinator assigned tasks to volunteers based on their availability and skills.News
  • ◆The manager assigned tasks clearly at the start of the week to avoid confusion later.Professional
  • ◆The team leader assigned tasks at the morning briefing so everyone knew their role for the day.Workplace
3 perform a task
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆With practice, even the most complicated tasks become easy to perform.General
  • ◆I can perform the task — I just need someone to show me once.Casual
  • ◆Rescue workers performed a series of difficult tasks under extremely dangerous conditions.News
  • ◆Employees must be fully trained before they are permitted to perform certain tasks independently.Professional
  • ◆New staff are supervised until they can perform all assigned tasks to the required standard.Workplace
4 carry out a task
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Breaking a big project into smaller pieces makes it much easier to carry out each task.General
  • ◆I'll carry out the task — just give me the instructions and I'll get it done.Casual
  • ◆The engineering team carried out a series of tasks to restore power to the affected region.News
  • ◆The consultants were brought in to carry out a full assessment of the existing processes.Professional
  • ◆Staff must carry out all tasks in accordance with the standard operating procedures.Workplace
