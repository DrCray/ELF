Handling Situations & Challenges

Lesson 24  ·  General Communication

B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 handle a situation
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆The way you handle a difficult situation says a lot about your character.General
  • ◆I didn't handle the situation well — I got emotional and said things I regret.Casual
  • ◆Paramedics are trained to handle situations that most people would find overwhelming.News
  • ◆The HR team handled the situation with professionalism and sensitivity.Professional
  • ◆The floor supervisor handled the situation calmly, preventing it from affecting the whole shift.Workplace
2 face a challenge
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Everyone faces challenges — what matters is how you respond to them.General
  • ◆I know it's hard, but you've faced bigger challenges than this before.Casual
  • ◆The country faces a significant challenge in rebuilding its infrastructure after the floods.News
  • ◆The new CFO faces the challenge of restoring investor confidence after two difficult years.Professional
  • ◆The team faces the challenge of maintaining output despite a shortage of raw materials.Workplace
3 overcome a challenge
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Looking back, the challenges you overcame are often the things you're most proud of.General
  • ◆You overcame that challenge — don't let anyone tell you it wasn't a big deal.Casual
  • ◆The expedition team overcame extraordinary challenges to reach the summit.News
  • ◆The company overcame significant logistical challenges to deliver the project on time.Professional
  • ◆The team overcame the challenge of retraining quickly and was back to full productivity within days.Workplace
4 find a solution
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆There's almost always a solution — you just have to be willing to look for it.General
  • ◆I don't care how we do it — I just need us to find a solution today.Casual
  • ◆Diplomats are working urgently to find a solution to the border dispute.News
  • ◆The working group was formed specifically to find a solution to the recurring compliance issue.Professional
  • ◆The maintenance team found a solution that kept the line running without a full shutdown.Workplace
★ Bonus

think on your feet

  • ◆Working in customer service teaches you to think on your feet in almost any situation.Workplace
  • ◆The best negotiators are those who can think on their feet when talks take an unexpected turn.Professional
