Example Sentences ▾

  • ◆The introduction of automated scanning had a major impact on processing times at the warehouse.Workplace

Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆People usually have a reason for behaving the way they do, even if it isn't obvious.General

Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆You don't have to change the world to make a difference — sometimes it starts with one person.General
  • ◆I know it feels small, but what you did today really did make a difference.Casual

  • ◆Simple improvements to the workstation layout can make a significant difference to daily comfort and output.Workplace
