Participation & Involvement

Lesson 22  ·  General Communication

B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 take part
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Everyone is welcome to take part, regardless of skill level.General
  • ◆Are you going to take part, or are you just going to watch again?Casual
  • ◆Over three thousand athletes took part in the regional championships.News
  • ◆All department heads are required to take part in the annual strategy review.Professional
  • ◆Staff are encouraged to take part in the monthly safety briefings.Workplace
2 get involved in
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆The best way to feel at home in a new city is to get involved in the local community.General
  • ◆How did you even get involved in something like that?Casual
  • ◆Several international organizations are getting involved in the peace negotiations.News
  • ◆Junior staff are encouraged to get involved in cross-departmental projects early in their careers.Professional
  • ◆Anyone interested in improving workplace conditions can get involved in the staff committee.Workplace
3 play a role in
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Regular exercise plays a significant role in maintaining both physical and mental health.General
  • ◆You played a role in this too — you can't pretend you had nothing to do with it.Casual
  • ◆Social media played a major role in shaping public opinion during the election.News
  • ◆Each team member plays a specific role in ensuring the project is delivered on time.Professional
  • ◆The line supervisor plays a key role in maintaining morale and productivity on the floor.Workplace
4 take advantage of
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆When an opportunity comes your way, take advantage of it before it disappears.General
  • ◆You should take advantage of the free training while it's still available.Casual
  • ◆Local businesses are being urged to take advantage of the new government grant scheme.News
  • ◆The company took advantage of the low interest rates to refinance its debt.Professional
  • ◆All employees are encouraged to take advantage of the professional development programs on offer.Workplace
★ Bonus

have a say in something

  • ◆Everyone on the team should have a say in how the workflow is organized.Workplace
  • ◆Citizens deserve to have a say in decisions that directly affect their daily lives.General
