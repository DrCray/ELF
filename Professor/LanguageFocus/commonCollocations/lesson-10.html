Collocation Mini-Lessons

Thinking It Through

Lesson 10  ·  General Communication

B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 think carefully
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Think carefully before you make a decision you can't undo.General
  • ◆Just think carefully, okay? Don't rush into it.Casual
  • ◆Lawmakers were urged to think carefully before voting on the controversial bill.News
  • ◆The board was advised to think carefully about the long-term implications of the merger.Professional
  • ◆Always think carefully before signing off on a safety checklist — it matters.Workplace
2 think seriously about
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆You should think seriously about going back to school.General
  • ◆I think you need to think seriously about what you actually want.Casual
  • ◆World leaders are being asked to think seriously about climate commitments.News
  • ◆The company needs to think seriously about its approach to staff retention.Professional
  • ◆Management is thinking seriously about investing in new equipment for the floor.Workplace
3 consider an option
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Before deciding, it's worth taking the time to consider every option.General
  • ◆Have you considered the option of just not going?Casual
  • ◆The committee considered several options before agreeing on a course of action.News
  • ◆The team was asked to consider all available options before presenting a recommendation.Professional
  • ◆The logistics manager considered the option of a third delivery shift to reduce backlog.Workplace
4 take something into consideration
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆When choosing a school, take the commute into consideration.General
  • ◆Can you take my schedule into consideration when you plan the trip?Casual
  • ◆The judge took several mitigating factors into consideration before sentencing.News
  • ◆All budget proposals must take current market conditions into consideration.Professional
  • ◆Shift assignments should take personal commitments into consideration where possible.Workplace
★ Bonus

weigh the options

  • ◆Before committing to anything, it's worth taking time to weigh the options carefully.General
  • ◆The project team spent the afternoon weighing the options before making a final recommendation.Professional
