Collocation Mini-Lessons

Making Decisions

Lesson 13  ·  General Communication

B1 – B2

Register Key

General Casual News Professional Workplace
4 phrases  ·  5 example sentences each  ·  See how each phrase is used across different real-world contexts.

1 make a decision
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆Sometimes you just have to make a decision and commit to it.General
  • ◆Can you just make a decision? We've been standing here for ten minutes.Casual
  • ◆The government made a decision to close the borders in response to the outbreak.News
  • ◆The leadership team made a strategic decision to shift focus to the Asian market.Professional
  • ◆The shift supervisor made a quick decision to halt production when the alarm sounded.Workplace
2 reach a decision
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆The family sat together for hours before they finally reached a decision.General
  • ◆Did you two reach a decision, or are you still arguing about it?Casual
  • ◆The jury reached a decision after four days of deliberation.News
  • ◆After lengthy negotiations, both parties reached a decision that satisfied everyone.Professional
  • ◆The management team reached a decision about the new break room schedule.Workplace
3 announce a decision
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆She announced her decision to leave the company after fifteen years.General
  • ◆Just announce the decision already — everyone's waiting.Casual
  • ◆The central bank announced its decision to raise interest rates by half a percent.News
  • ◆The CEO announced the decision to restructure the company at an all-hands meeting.Professional
  • ◆The HR department announced the decision to introduce flexible working hours.Workplace
4 reverse a decision
Example Sentences ▾
  • ◆He reversed his decision after hearing how much it had upset his family.General
  • ◆Wait — you're reversing the decision now? After all that?Casual
  • ◆The court reversed its earlier decision following a successful appeal.News
  • ◆Under pressure from stakeholders, the board reversed its decision to cut the training budget.Professional
  • ◆The operations manager reversed the decision to eliminate the quality check step.Workplace
★ Bonus

stand by a decision

  • ◆Despite the criticism, she stood by her decision and explained her reasoning clearly.General
  • ◆The director stood by the decision, insisting the data fully supported the new direction.Professional
