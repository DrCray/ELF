English Idioms Mini-Lessons
Give Notice & Noted (B2-C1 Level)

1. Give Notice

To formally inform your employer that you are resigning from your job, usually with advance warning (e.g., two weeks' notice).
Examples:
  • She gave her two weeks' notice yesterday and starts her new job next month.
  • I'll give notice at the end of the month if they don't improve my working conditions.

Quick Quiz: What does "give notice" mean in a work context?

To warn about a problem
To resign from a job
To start a new job
To give a presentation

2. Noted

A concise way to say "I understand" or "got it" in professional or casual conversations.
Examples:
  • Boss: Meeting at 3 PM.
    Employee: Noted.
  • Friend: The party starts at 8.
    You: Noted, see you then!
