Lingering

Lingering smell: The lingering smell of coffee filled the room long after the pot was empty.

Definition

Lingering (adj.): lasting for a long time; persisting.

It comes from the verb linger, meaning to stay longer than expected or to move slowly.

We lingered over coffee after dinner, talking about our plans for the summer.

Examples

She had a lingering feeling of sadness after the movie ended.

The lingering effects of the cold kept him in bed for days.

There was a lingering silence after her unexpected announcement.

He couldn’t shake the lingering doubt about his decision.

The garden had a lingering beauty even in autumn.
