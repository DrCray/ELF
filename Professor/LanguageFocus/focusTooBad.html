“Too Bad”

An extremely versatile expression

It can mean anything from genuine sympathy ("I'm sorry to hear that") to complete indifference ("I don't care" / "Tough luck").

The exact meaning depends on tone, facial expression, and context.

🙏 Use 1: Sympathy / mild regret kind · polite
Too bad about your grandma being in hospital. How is she doing?
Too bad your flight got cancelled.
Too bad you didn’t get the job. You were so prepared!
Too bad we ran out of ice cream.
Too bad your team lost again this season.

Short natural responses

A: I sprained my ankle playing football.
B: That’s too bad. Does it hurt a lot?
A: My cat ran away yesterday.
B: Oh, that’s too bad. Have you put up posters?

Longer versions

It’s too bad that the concert was sold out.
It’s too bad you have to move to another city for work.
It was too bad nobody came to the surprise party.
🔇 Use 2: Hard‑line / dismissive cold · flat · no pity
A: Can I borrow your charger? Mine died.
B: Too bad. I need it. Next time bring your own.
A: Please let me copy your homework!
B: Too bad. Do your own work.
A: I really need this favor.
B: Too bad – not my problem.
A: The shop closed five minutes ago!
B: Too bad. Come earlier next time.
🧊 said seriously or flatly — often rude or cold
😄 Use 3: Joking / banter playful · teasing
A: I wanted the last slice of pizza!
B: Too bad! You snooze, you lose.
A: I lost at Mario Kart again…
B: Too bad! Better luck next time!
A: Hey, save me some popcorn!
B: Too bad – I just ate the last bite.

🎮 Games, sports, friendly competition

[Your friend misses a basketball shot.]
   Too bad, so sad!
[Your friend draws a Monopoly “Go to Jail” card.]
   Too bad. Your loss, my gain.
[Your friend loses a life in a video game.]
   Aw, too bad! You were doing so well. (sarcasm)
🙃 said with a smile, laugh, or shrug — all in good fun
🌧️ Use 4: Literal “too bad” not idiomatic

Describes a situation/thing that is really bad / impossible because of the condition.

The weather is too bad to go cycling today.
This milk is too bad – it smells awful.
The signal here is too bad to make a video call.
My cough is too bad to go to school.
The traffic is too bad – we’ll be late.
