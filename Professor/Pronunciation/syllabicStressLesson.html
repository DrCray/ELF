Section E: Extended Practice on Word-Families

with differentiating stress patterns

Some word-families carry the same syllabic stress across all its members. For example:

  • manufacturer | manufacturing | manufacturable
  • efficient | efficiency | efficiently

However, this is not the case with many word families.
