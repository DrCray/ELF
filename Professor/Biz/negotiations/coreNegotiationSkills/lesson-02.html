Lesson 2: Identifying Constraints and Limits

Key Phrases

Basic patterns:

  • We're limited by [constraint]
  • That would be difficult because [reason]
  • Unfortunately, we're not able to [action]
  • We don't have much flexibility on [issue]
  • This falls outside [boundary / policy]

Additional expressions:

  • We're constrained by [limitation]
  • It would be challenging to [action]
  • We wouldn't be able to [action] because [reason]
  • There's limited room for [change]
  • We're restricted by [policy / budget / timeline]
  • That creates challenges for us
  • This poses significant challenges for [process / production]

Example Sentences

  • We're limited by our current production capacity.
  • That would be difficult because of our existing commitments.
  • Unfortunately, we're not able to extend the payment period beyond 45 days.
  • We don't have much flexibility on pricing due to fixed costs.
  • This falls outside what we're able to approve under current policy.
  • We're constrained by internal regulations.
  • It would be challenging to adjust the scope under the current agreement.
  • We wouldn't be able to meet that deadline because of resource limitations.
  • There's limited room for movement on delivery dates.
  • Moving to higher specifications would pose significant challenges for our production process.

Dialog: Deadlines & Delivery Timelines

Buyer: At this stage, our main concern is the delivery schedule.

Supplier: We're limited by our current production capacity.

Buyer: Could we explore whether there's room to move on the delivery date?

Supplier: We might be able to reduce delivery by one week; however, we wouldn't be able to go beyond that.

Buyer: That sounds reasonable. This would help us meet our internal deadlines.

Supplier: Then it sounds like we're aligned on the revised timeline.

Variation:

Buyer: Our main concern at the moment is the delivery schedule of 8 weeks.

Supplier: We are limited by our production capacity right now.

Buyer: Is there any possibility to bring the delivery date forward?

Supplier: We could perhaps reduce it by one week, to 7 weeks, but we cannot do more than that because of current orders.

Buyer: That seems acceptable. It would help us meet our own internal deadlines for assembly.

Supplier: Good. Then we are agreed on the new timeline of 7 weeks.
