Lesson 1: Stating a Position

Key Phrases

Basic patterns:

  • Our position is [noun phrase]
  • From our side, [priority / requirement]
  • At this stage, we're looking for [goal / outcome]
  • Our main concern is [issue]
  • From our perspective, [statement]

Additional expressions:

  • The key issue is [noun phrase]
  • Our priority is [goal]
  • What we need is [requirement]
  • We're focused on [objective]
  • The most important thing for us is [priority]

Example Sentences

  • Our position is that the delivery timeline needs to be confirmed.
  • From our side, the key issue is budget constraints this quarter.
  • At this stage, we're looking for a more flexible payment schedule.
  • Our main concern is meeting internal quality standards.
  • From our perspective, the current price doesn't work for us.
  • The key issue is maintaining consistency across batches.
  • Our priority is ensuring the project stays within the agreed timeline.
  • What we need is clear confirmation on the delivery date.

Dialog: Price & Cost Adjustment

Buyer: From our side, the current price doesn't work for us.

Seller: I understand. Unfortunately, we don't have much flexibility here due to internal cost requirements.

Buyer: The reason for our concern is budget pressure this quarter. Would you be open to adjusting the price slightly?

Seller: We wouldn't be able to reduce the price, but we could offer a faster delivery.

Buyer: That could work, provided the delivery timeline is confirmed.

Seller: Great. So we're agreed on faster delivery at the current price.

Variation:

Buyer: The price of $25 per unit is too high for us now.

Seller: I understand. We cannot change the price much because our costs for materials like aluminum are higher this year.

Buyer: We have budget problems this quarter. Can you make the price a little lower?

Seller: We cannot lower the price, but we can send the order faster, in 4 weeks instead of 6.

Buyer: That is good, if you can promise the delivery date.

Seller: Yes. So we agree on faster delivery at $25 per unit.
