7-Unit Series

Modern Workplace English

Real conversations  ·  CEFR B1–B2  ·  American English

Seven units built around one office, one team, and the everyday situations that bring them together — and sometimes apart. Each unit features a main dialogue, vocabulary, mini-dialogues, comprehension questions, a language focus section, and discussion prompts.

Unit 1 The Breakroom Stale coffee, missing lunches, and a cracked pot — Marcus, Priya, and Dev take stock of their breakroom situation and start making plans. → Unit 2 Big News Theres' a rumor that Director Parker is retiring. The team reacts. → Unit 3 The Job Posting The director position is posted internally. Priya suggests that Dev should apply? →
Unit 4 The Team Meeting The whole team gathers to discuss the transition. Not everyone agrees on how to move forward. →
Unit 5 Working from Home A new policy lands in everyone's inbox. Two days remote, three in the office. Reactions are mixed. →
Unit 6 The Difficult Client An important client is unhappy with the latest project deliverable. Someone has to make the call. →
Unit 7 Parker's Last Day Cake, speeches, and a few honest goodbyes. The team says farewell — and looks ahead. →
