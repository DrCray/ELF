Dictation/Summaries for "Beautiful Year" Just4you Ad Campaign

Note: The dictations below double as a summary for each lesson.
For instructors: Have the students look over the 7 sentences. Then choose 3 or 4 to give as a dictation.

Why dictations? In short, the combination of auditory, visual, and tactile learning increases retention.

Lesson 1 Marketing Strategy

Dictation audioVid 1
Dictation Check
  1. The marketing team at Beautiful Years needs to promote their new cosmetics line, Just4you, designed for middle-aged women.
  2. To stand out, they suggest personalized beauty consultations and partnerships with beauty influencers.
  3. Organizing a beauty event with local celebrities could create excitement and attract attention to the new product line.
  4. Exclusive discounts and limited-edition gift sets will generate buzz and encourage customers to try their products.
  5. Building strong customer relationships and providing excellent service are key to long-term success.
  6. Social media campaigns, like user-generated content and contests, will help spread the word about Just3you.
  7. Patience and persistence are essential, as success in marketing takes time.

Lesson 2 Overcoming Resistance

Lesson 4 Mall Promotion

Dictation Check
  1. The team plans a mall promotion with free makeovers to introduce their healthier skincare products to resistant middle-aged customers.
  2. A dedicated booth or pop-up beauty station in a high-traffic area will attract visitors and demonstrate the products’ transformative effects.

Lesson 6 Partnership Strategy

Lesson 7 Ethical Concerns

Dictation audioVid 7
Dictation Check
  1. Jenny Rodrigez’s representative raises ethical concerns about child labor, environmental practices, and animal testing.
  2. The team prepares to address these concerns by presenting strict policies against child labor and compliance with international labor standards.

  5. Transparent communication, including documentation and third-party endorsements, will provide tangible evidence of their ethical commitment.
  6. The team proposes joint charitable initiatives focused on child welfare, environmental conservation, and animal rights.
  7. They express willingness to adjust practices to align with Jenny’s values and build a strong, ethical partnership.

Lesson 9 Contract Negotiations

Dictation audioVid 9
Dictation Check
  1. Sally and Charles discuss payment options, considering a flat rate, percentage of sales, and stock options.
  2. Stock options are dismissed due to concerns about Jenny appearing too affiliated with the company.
  3. They agree on a percentage fee for annual endorsement of four specific products: serum, contours, eye shadow, and foundation.
  4. The contract will be renewable annually by mutual agreement, allowing flexibility and performance evaluation.
  5. Charles requests complimentary products for Jenny as part of the deal.
  6. Sally agrees to include regular, complimentary product distribution in the contract.
  7. The agreement ensures fair compensation tied to product success and maintains Jenny’s independent status.
