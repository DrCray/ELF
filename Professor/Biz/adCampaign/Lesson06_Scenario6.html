Lesson 6: Project Leadership and Contact Strategy

Scenario 6: Managing Celebrity Endorsement Process

Scenario Overview

The team discusses the next steps for securing Jenny Rodrigez as their celebrity endorser. They assign marketing manager Sarah to lead the project and assemble a dedicated team with expertise in celebrity partnerships, contract negotiations, and public relations. The discussion covers the process of contacting Jenny through professional talent agencies, preparing a comprehensive proposal highlighting brand alignment, researching her recent projects and interests, and maintaining professional communication throughout. The team emphasizes the importance of professionalism, persistence, flexibility, and transparent communication to successfully navigate complex celebrity endorsement negotiations.

Participants: Mark Stratem (Product Leader), Leila Simpson (Packaging Designer), Bert Thomas (R&D), Tina Tarter (Product Distribution)
