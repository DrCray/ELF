Lesson 13B: Vocabulary

Business Vocabulary from Scenario 13 (B1+ Level)

This lesson presents important business vocabulary used in Scenario 13. Each word is defined and illustrated with an example sentence relevant to business contexts.

Viability
Definition: The ability to work successfully; the practicality or feasibility of a plan or idea.
Example: We need to assess the viability of the project before committing resources.
Immense
Definition: Extremely large or great in size, degree, or extent.
Example: The new market represents an immense opportunity for expansion.
Evident
Definition: Clear and obvious; easily seen or understood.
Example: It was evident from the data that the marketing campaign was successful.
Navigate
Definition: To find a way through or manage a complex situation successfully.
Example: Experienced managers know how to navigate challenging negotiations.
Feasibility
Definition: The state or degree of being easily or conveniently done; practicality.
Example: The team conducted a feasibility study before launching the new product line.
Coordination
Definition: The organization of different elements or activities to enable them to work together effectively.
Example: Successful project management requires excellent coordination among team members.
Accommodates
Definition: To provide what is needed or make adjustments for; to adapt to or make room for.
Example: Our flexible schedule accommodates employees with different working preferences.
Adequately
Definition: To a satisfactory or sufficient degree; in a way that meets requirements.
Example: We need to ensure all employees are adequately trained before implementing the new system.
