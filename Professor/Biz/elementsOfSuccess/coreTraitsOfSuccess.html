Core Traits of Success
[opinion piece]
Getting Started
  • Discuss how it is possible that lottery winners of millions of dollars can go broke in just a few years.

There are many paths to success. Be cautious when people say that you MUST follow specific rules for success. There have been many successful people who simply had a good idea at the right time and in the right place and with the right people.

Nevertheless, there never was a lazy person who achieved success on their own, nor keep success once they had it.

Here are some other useful traits that many successful people have.

1. Self-Discipline: They do what needs to be done, even when they don't feel like it. They maintain a strong work ethic. They stay focused and eliminate distractions.

2. Personal responsibility: They acknowledge their mistakes and learn from them, rather than blaming external factors or other people.

3. Proactive: They take action on their own without waiting for others to direct them or bring them what they need.

4. Positivity: They keep a positive attitude most of the time. When they have a bad day, they bounce back quickly. They control their own emotions rather than letting the actions or attitudes of others affect them.

5. Resilience: They see failures as stepping stones to success. Setbacks are just a part of life. Challenges are a chance to improve or to try a different approach.

6. Flexibility: They recognize that there is no straight line to success. They adapt and adjust when necessary instead of giving up.

7. Continuous Learning: A commitment to lifelong learning leads to continuous improvement. They are curious and open to new ideas and perspectives.

  3. Explain proactive and give examples.
  4. Explain positivity and give examples.

  6. Explain flexibility and give examples.
  7. Explain continuous learning and give examples.
