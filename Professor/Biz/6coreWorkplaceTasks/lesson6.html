LESSON 6

Giving an Opinion Diplomatically

Main Page Lesson 1 Lesson 2 Lesson 3 Lesson 4 Lesson 5 Lesson 6

Express your views tactfully while respecting others' perspectives and maintaining professionalism.

1. "From my perspective..."
From my perspective, we should prioritize user experience over features.
From my perspective, the timing isn't ideal for this initiative.
From my perspective, we need more data before making a decision.
From my perspective, the current approach is too resource-intensive.
From my perspective, we're underestimating the complexity involved.
From my perspective, this could position us well for future opportunities.
2. "I tend to think that..."
I tend to think that a gradual approach would be more sustainable.
I tend to think that we should involve the team earlier in the process.
I tend to think that the benefits outweigh the risks in this case.
I tend to think that we're setting expectations too high initially.
I tend to think that outsourcing might compromise quality control.
I tend to think that this aligns well with our core competencies.
3. "In my experience..."
In my experience, these implementations typically take longer than planned.
In my experience, stakeholder buy-in is critical for success.
In my experience, investing in training yields significant returns.
In my experience, rushing the testing phase leads to problems later.
In my experience, clear metrics help keep projects on track.
In my experience, early wins build momentum for larger changes.
4. "I see the merits of that approach, but..."
I see the merits of that approach, but we might face capacity constraints.
I see the merits of that approach, but timing could be challenging.
I see the merits of that approach, but it may not scale effectively.
I see the merits of that approach, but costs could escalate quickly.
I see the merits of that approach, but implementation complexity concerns me.
I see the merits of that approach, but stakeholder alignment might be difficult.
5. "It seems to me that..."
It seems to me that we're focusing on symptoms rather than root causes.
It seems to me that customer feedback should weigh more heavily here.
It seems to me that we need clearer decision-making criteria.
It seems to me that the risks are manageable with proper planning.
It seems to me that we're duplicating efforts across departments.
It seems to me that a pilot program would provide valuable insights.
6. "I'd respectfully suggest that..."
I'd respectfully suggest that we reconsider the timeline for this project.
I'd respectfully suggest that we seek input from the operations team.
I'd respectfully suggest that we explore alternative vendors.
I'd respectfully suggest that we clarify roles and responsibilities first.
I'd respectfully suggest that we address the technical debt before adding features.
I'd respectfully suggest that we align this with the quarterly objectives.
