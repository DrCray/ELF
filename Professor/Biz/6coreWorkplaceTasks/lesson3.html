LESSON 3

Making a Suggestion

Main Page Lesson 1 Lesson 2 Lesson 3 Lesson 4 Lesson 5 Lesson 6

Discover professional ways to propose ideas, offer solutions, and recommend actions.

1. "I'd like to propose that we..."
I'd like to propose that we implement a monthly review process.
I'd like to propose that we switch to a cloud-based solution.
I'd like to propose that we extend the trial period by 30 days.
I'd like to propose that we bring in an external consultant.
I'd like to propose that we standardize our reporting format.
I'd like to propose that we establish a backup supplier relationship.
2. "One option would be to..."
One option would be to outsource the data entry work.
One option would be to stagger the implementation across regions.
One option would be to negotiate a longer payment term.
One option would be to run a pilot program before full rollout.
One option would be to consolidate these three positions into two.
One option would be to invest in automation for this process.
3. "Have we considered...?"
Have we considered partnering with a local distributor?
Have we considered moving the event to a virtual format?
Have we considered offering flexible work arrangements?
Have we considered a phased approach to the launch?
Have we considered cross-training staff to provide coverage?
Have we considered revising our pricing structure?
4. "It might be worth trying..."
It might be worth trying a different marketing channel.
It might be worth trying to renegotiate the contract terms.
It might be worth trying a simplified version first.
It might be worth trying to source materials domestically.
It might be worth trying to bundle these services together.
It might be worth trying a weekly check-in instead of monthly.
5. "What if we were to...?"
What if we were to shift resources from Project A to Project B?
What if we were to extend our operating hours?
What if we were to offer early-bird pricing?
What if we were to combine these two departments?
What if we were to hire contractors for the peak season?
What if we were to revisit our original timeline?
6. "My recommendation would be to..."
My recommendation would be to prioritize the high-impact items first.
My recommendation would be to secure additional funding before proceeding.
My recommendation would be to conduct a thorough risk assessment.
My recommendation would be to seek input from the customer service team.
My recommendation would be to postpone the launch until Q2.
My recommendation would be to invest in preventive maintenance now.
