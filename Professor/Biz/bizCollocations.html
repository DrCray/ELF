B1 High Value Phrases Everyone Should Know

Essential collocations for professional communication.
(General use, Workplace, and News)

Business & Workplace Actions

  • Make a decision
  • Reach a decision
  • Take action
  • Take steps
View Examples

After three hours, the team finally managed to reach a decision.

The CEO will take steps to reduce plastic waste.

If we follow a strategy, we are more likely to succeed.
