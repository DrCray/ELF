Toner and  serum


Toner helps balance your skin's PH. It has the feel of water so it comes in bigger bottles. You will apply a handful.

Serum is a thick liquid. You only need a tiny bit, like a small drop or two.
This will go deep into your skin. The skin absorbs it quickly.






Lotion and Moisturizer (cream)


Lotion is much thicker than toner, but is not as thick as moisturizing cream. It does not absorb as quickly as serum.

Moisturizing cream is the most thick of all. It works like lotion but also helps to create a barrier to lock in moisture.







Foundation and Concealer

Foundation is the first layer of makeup that you put on. It's purpose is to help even your skin tone. It is not meant to replace your natural skin tone.  So try and keep your foundation color as close to your natural skin color as possible. A slight shade or two darker or lighter is okay.

Concealer is thicker than foundation. It is meant to cover blemishes, skin spots, and dark circles around/under the eyes.






Contouring, Bronzing and Highlighting

Contouring is blending shades of colors to enhance your faces natural shape (structure).  Some people call this sculpting.

Contouring has a matte effect which means it is not shiny or glossy. The light effect is very flat, the shadow effect is very strong.


Contour Palette



Bronzing, on the other hand, is a very shiny or glossy, glowing look. The light effect is very strong.


Bronzing Palette / Bronzing Shades


Highlighter

(highlighting)

Use for adding that bit of extra light-reflection to the highest areas of your face. Give your cheekboness that extra lift.






Mascara, Eyeliner, and Eyeshadow

Mascara: This is used to give the look of fuller eyelashes.

Just a little bit of mascara on your eyelashes can give you more volume and make them appear longer and fuller.


Eyeliner: This is used to line the eye, on the top as well as the bottom. This can help your eyes to appear bigger.


Eyeshadow: This is put on the eyelids to add a bit of color, and perhaps some shimmer,








Lip liner, Lipstick, and Lip gloss


Lipliner: You use this to line the lips and give them a fuller look.

Put this on before your lipstick. It will blend better.



Lipstick: This brings color or shimmer or definition to your lips.



Lip gloss: This is a shinier substitute or addition to lipstick.






Blush: This is used sparingly on the cheeks.





Eyebrow pencil: This helps to fill in any gaps in the eyebrows.

Thai massage

Thai massage is a type of massage therapy that combines acupressure, yoga, and stretching. It is a traditional Thai healing art that has been practiced for centuries.

Thai massage is performed on a mat on the floor, with the client fully clothed. The therapist uses their hands, feet, and elbows to apply pressure to the body's energy lines, known as sen. The therapist may also use stretching and yoga-like movements to help the client relax and release tension.

Thai massage is a gentle yet effective way to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote relaxation. It can also help to improve flexibility, reduce stress, and boost energy levels.

Some of the potential benefits of Thai massage include:

  • Reduced muscle tension
  • Improved circulation
  • Increased flexibility
  • Reduced stress
  • Improved sleep
  • Boosted energy levels
  • Enhanced relaxation

If you are considering a Thai massage, it is important to find a qualified massage therapist. A qualified massage therapist will have received training in Thai massage techniques and will be able to tailor the massage to your individual needs.

Here are some of the key differences between Thai massage and Swedish massage:

  • Thai massage is performed on a mat on the floor, while Swedish massage is typically performed on a massage table.
  • Thai massage uses acupressure, yoga, and stretching, while Swedish massage focuses on long, gliding strokes, kneading, friction, tapping, and vibration.
  • Thai massage is typically more vigorous than Swedish massage.
  • Thai massage can be performed on clothed clients, while Swedish massage is typically performed on unclothed clients.

Both Thai massage and Swedish massage can be beneficial for relaxation and improved health and well-being. The best type of massage for you will depend on your individual needs and preferences.

Swedish Massage

A Swedish massage is a type of massage therapy that uses long, gliding strokes, kneading, friction, tapping, and vibration to promote relaxation and improve circulation. It is one of the most common types of massage therapy in the world.

The four basic strokes used in Swedish massage are:

  • Effleurage: Long, gliding strokes that are used to warm up the muscles and prepare them for deeper work.
  • Petrissage: Kneading movements that are used to break up muscle tension and improve circulation.
  • Friction: Deep, circular movements that are used to break down scar tissue and improve flexibility.
  • Tapotement: Light tapping or drumming movements that are used to stimulate the nervous system and improve circulation.

In addition to these four basic strokes, Swedish massage therapists may also use other techniques, such as vibration, stretching, and joint mobilization.

Swedish massage is a safe and effective way to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote relaxation. It can be beneficial for people of all ages and fitness levels.

Some of the potential benefits of Swedish massage include:

  • Reduced stress and anxiety

It takes more than a pair of scissors to give you a whole new look. If you want to dye your hair or get some highlights, the colorist will most likely need a stylist apron and a pair of disposable gloves, to protect them from the chemicals.

Also, they will need hair dye or color tubes, bleaching powder, and a measuring cup to create the color that will be used in your hair. They will also need a dye/color bowl and dye/color brushes. 

After working on the color of your hair, you will need to get your hair washed and styled. When you go to the washing station, the hairdresser will apply shampoo and conditioner to your hair. They will then need a comb or a brush to detangle the hair. They may use a hair dryer, a curling iron, or a flat iron/straightener to style it. Hair clips are also helpful when styling the hair.
