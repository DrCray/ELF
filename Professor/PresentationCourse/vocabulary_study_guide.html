Chocolate: King of Treats

Vocabulary Study Guide

CEFR B1 Level • High-Intermediate English Vocabulary Practice

Context Note: These definitions and example sentences match exactly how the words are used in the article about chocolate. Use these cards to study their specific meanings.
mood-enhancing adjective
Definition
Making someone feel happier, more relaxed, or in a better emotional state.
Example Sentences
  • Many people listen to upbeat music for its mood-enhancing effects on a rainy day.
  • Walking in a sunny park has a natural mood-enhancing quality that reduces stress.
versatility noun
Definition
The quality of being useful in many different ways or able to do many different things.
Example Sentences
  • The chef loves the versatility of potatoes because they can be baked, fried, or mashed.
  • A smartphone is famous for its versatility, acting as a phone, camera, and computer all at once.
indulgence noun
Definition
An expensive or special treat that you allow yourself to enjoy, even if you do not need it.
Example Sentences
  • Buying a ticket to a luxury spa was a wonderful weekend indulgence for Sarah.
  • An extra piece of strawberry cake is a small indulgence after a long week of hard work.
fascinating adjective
Definition
Extremely interesting or attractive; holding your attention completely.
Example Sentences
  • The students found the documentary about deep-sea creatures absolutely fascinating.
  • History books are full of fascinating stories about ancient queens and kings.
originated verb (past tense)
Definition
Started or came from a specific place, time, or situation.
Example Sentences
  • The popular dish pizza originated in Italy before spreading across the globe.
  • Scientists believe that this specific type of plant originated in the rainforests of South America.
revered verb (past tense / adjective)
Definition
Respected and admired deeply; treated as highly important or sacred.
Example Sentences
  • The ancient elders were revered by the village people for their wisdom.
  • In many traditional cultures, older teachers are deeply revered by their students.
consumed verb (past tense)
Definition
Eaten, drunk, or used up.
Example Sentences
  • The hot soup was quickly consumed by the cold hikers when they reached the cabin.
  • Large amounts of coffee are consumed every morning in busy city offices.
transforming verb (present participle)
Definition
Changing something completely, especially in a way that improves its appearance or character.
Example Sentences
  • The artist is transforming the old, gray wall into a beautiful colorful mural.
  • Adding fresh herbs is an easy way of transforming a simple pasta dish into a gourmet meal.
sensation noun
Definition
Something that causes a lot of excitement, interest, or public attention worldwide.
Example Sentences
  • The young singer became an overnight sensation after her video went viral.
  • The new smartphone app created a global sensation, gaining millions of users in a week.
remarkable adjective
Definition
Unusual, special, or surprising in a way that causes people to notice or praise it.
Example Sentences
  • The young child showed a remarkable talent for playing the piano without lessons.
  • It is remarkable how much the city has changed and grown over the last ten years.
enhance verb
Definition
To improve the quality, amount, value, or strength of something.
Example Sentences
  • You can enhance the flavor of the soup by adding a little bit of salt and pepper.
  • The new lighting system was designed to enhance the beauty of the paintings in the museum.
stimulate verb
Definition
To make an organ or body system become more active, or to encourage a reaction.
Example Sentences
  • Bright colors like yellow and orange can stimulate the brain and increase energy levels.
  • The exercise session helped to stimulate his blood circulation and wake him up.
texture noun
Definition
The way that something feels when you touch it or eat it (e.g., smooth, rough, soft).
Example Sentences
  • This cream has a very light, smooth texture that absorbs into the skin quickly.
  • The bread had a wonderful crunchy texture on the outside but was soft inside.
aroma noun
Definition
A pleasant, distinctive smell, usually from food, drink, or plants.
Example Sentences
  • The rich aroma of fresh coffee filled the kitchen early in the morning.
  • We walked into the bakery, drawn in by the sweet aroma of warm cinnamon rolls.
sensory adjective
Definition
Connected with the physical senses (sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch).
Example Sentences
  • Walking through the flower market offers a wonderful sensory experience of bright colors and smells.
  • Wine tasting is a complete sensory process involving sight, aroma, and taste.
enduring adjective
Definition
Lasting for a long time; continuing to exist or be popular despite difficulties.
Example Sentences
  • The classic novel has an enduring popularity that has lasted for over a century.
  • Grandparents often share an enduring love that serves as a beautiful example for the family.
significance noun
Definition
The importance or special meaning of something, especially because of its effects.
Example Sentences
  • The historical monument holds a huge cultural significance for the local community.
  • Doctors explained the deep significance of getting enough sleep every single night.
elaborate adjective
Definition
Detailed, complicated, or carefully planned with many parts.
Example Sentences
  • The dancers wore elaborate costumes covered in shiny beads and colorful feathers.
  • They prepared an elaborate surprise party with a complete schedule of events.
combination noun
Definition
Two or more different things joined, mixed, or used together.
Example Sentences
  • The salad is a delicious combination of sweet apples, crunchy walnuts, and fresh lettuce.
  • Success in learning a language requires a combination of regular practice and patience.
relevance noun
Definition
The quality of being directly connected, important, or useful to a specific situation or topic.
Example Sentences
  • The teacher explained the historical relevance of the old treaty to today's political situation.
  • Please only ask questions that have direct relevance to the topic we are studying today.
appeal noun
Definition
The quality of being attractive, interesting, or popular to a lot of people.
Example Sentences
  • The universal appeal of animated movies means both children and parents can enjoy them.
  • Simple video games often have a wide appeal because they are easy to understand and play.
