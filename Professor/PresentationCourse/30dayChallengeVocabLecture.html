Vocabulary Review

  1. Stuck in a rut

  • Meaning: Feeling trapped in a dull or monotonous routine.

  • Example sentence: After years of working the same job, she felt stuck in a rut, longing for a change in scenery and career.

  1. Follow in the footsteps

  • Meaning: To emulate someone's actions or choices.

  • Example sentence: Inspired by her grandmother's adventurous spirit, she decided to follow in her footsteps and travel the world after retirement.

  1. Philosopher

  • Meaning: A person who seeks wisdom and knowledge through intellectual exploration.

  • Example sentence: The ancient Greek philosopher Socrates is known for his profound teachings on ethics and the pursuit of truth.

  1. Subtract

  • Meaning: To remove or take away something.

  • Example sentence: To improve her health, she decided to subtract sugary snacks from her daily diet.

  1. Challenges

  • Meaning: Difficult tasks or goals set to achieve.

  • Example sentence: Overcoming language challenges became easier for him after attending intensive English classes.

  1. Flying by

  • Meaning: Passing quickly without being noticed or remembered.

  • Example sentence: The weekend flew by as they enjoyed their time camping in the mountains.

  1. Memorable

  • Meaning: Something worth remembering or easily recalled.

  • Example sentence: Their wedding day was truly memorable, with family and friends celebrating under the stars.

  1. Self-confidence

  • Meaning: Belief in oneself and one's abilities.

  • Example sentence: Building self-confidence is essential for students preparing to enter the job market.

  1. Desk-dwelling

  • Meaning: Spending most of one's time at a desk.

  • Example sentence: As a desk-dwelling accountant, she appreciated the chance to attend outdoor yoga sessions on weekends.

  1. Nerd

  • Meaning: A person with a strong interest in intellectual pursuits or specific fields.

  • Example sentence: He's a computer nerd who loves spending weekends coding new software projects.

  1. Adventurous

  • Meaning: Willing to take risks or try new experiences.

  • Example sentence: Despite her cautious nature, she surprised everyone by booking an adventurous skydiving trip.

  1. Figured out

  • Meaning: To understand or solve something.

  • Example sentence: After several attempts, she finally figured out how to use the new software program effectively.

  1. Novel

  • Meaning: A long fictional prose narrative.

  • Example sentence: Writing a novel has always been his dream since childhood.

  1. Sleep-deprived

  • Meaning: Lacking adequate sleep.

  • Example sentence: During exam week, students often feel sleep-deprived due to late-night study sessions.

  1. Awful

  • Meaning: Very bad or unpleasant.

  • Example sentence: Despite the chef's effort, the dinner turned out awful due to overcooked pasta.

  1. Novelist

  • Meaning: An author of novels.

  • Example sentence: She aspires to become a successful novelist and has already started working on her first book.

  1. Sustainable

  • Meaning: Capable of being maintained or continued over the long term.

  • Example sentence: The company focuses on developing sustainable farming practices to protect the environment.

  1. To stick

  • Meaning: To remain or continue to be effective.

  • Example sentence: Learning vocabulary daily helps to stick new words in long-term memory.

  1. Ton of fun

  • Meaning: A large amount of enjoyment or pleasure.

  • Example sentence: The carnival was a ton of fun with its thrilling rides and delicious food stalls.

  1. Less likely

  • Meaning: Not probable or unlikely.

  • Example sentence: With proper care and maintenance, older cars are less likely to experience major mechanical issues.

  1. Guarantee

  • Meaning: A promise or assurance, typically with certainty.

  • Example sentence: The manufacturer guarantees the quality of their products with a 10-year warranty.

  1. Give it a shot

  • Meaning: To try something, often with uncertain outcomes.

  • Example sentence: Encouraged by friends, he decided to give surfing a shot during his vacation in Hawaii.
