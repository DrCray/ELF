Basic Instructions
  • Triple-click text to edit any part of this form.
  • click add quSet to add another question/choices set. Edit the question set.
  • The answer: must match the correct-choice exactly for automatic scoring to work, although the number of spaces is not relevant.
    • For example "blue dog" " blue dog " "     b lu e   do g    " will all match correctly for scoring;
    • Misspelled or missing charactars are impossible to match,
      e.g. "blu dog" "blue dog" "bleu dog" will not match.
  • When you are finished, clicking Save/Download will save and download the new test as an html file.
    • Option1: Send the file via email, group chat, or other social media to your students. They can open it in their web browser (tested on Chrome and Firefox).
    • Option2: Post it online on your website. Give students the link and they can take the test online.
    • Option 3: Put the html file on your cloud server or other file-sharing server, give students the link, and the students can download it and open it in their browser.
  • When your students finish the exam, they can click on print and save the file as PDF and send to you via email, group chat, social media or other file-sharing method.

    • The score is at the top of the page in the coded message (explained below). Just open the pdf and record the score in your gradebook.
    • Telegram Messenger can be on your phone AND desktop so it's easier to download and manage PDFs on your computer. It's max file size is a full 2GB.
    • Optional: Use a separate free email account for ONLY students/classes. You can tell students to put a code in the subject line and then set your email/junk filter to only allow those emails.
  • The score is hidden in the alphanumeric code at the top of the pdf page.
    • It looks something like this: #async@http832.6ii8n.56i.url.vim .
    • In the above example, the score is 86.
    • It is the numbers on either side of the ii .
      "#async@http832 .6ii8.56i.url.vim"
    • The numbers are transposed transposed (reversed). So .6ii8. = 86 .
    • The code is randomly generated each time,
      but the score will always be in the same place.
      e.g. .7ii6. = 67 .4ii5. = 54 .7ii9. = 97 etc.
    • 00i1 is 100; 0i0 is 0; 7i0 is 07, or just 7.
      .00ii1. = 100 .0ii0. = 0 .7ii0. = 07 .
