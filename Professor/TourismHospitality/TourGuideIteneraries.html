New York City itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

  • 8:00 AM - Breakfast: Enjoy a classic New York breakfast at the famous Sarabeth's, known for its delicious brunch offerings.

  • 10:00 AM - Morning: Arrival in New York City and check-in at the hotel.

  • 1:00 PM - Afternoon: Explore the local neighborhood and get acquainted with the surroundings.

  • 2:30 PM - Lunch: Grab a quick and tasty lunch at Gray's Papaya, a popular hot dog street vendor.

  • 7:00 PM - Evening: Enjoy a welcome dinner at a classic New York City restaurant.

  • 10:00 PM - Nightlife: Experience the trendy nightlife in the Meatpacking District with its bars and clubs. Check out popular spots such as The Standard Biergarten for a vibrant beer garden atmosphere or Le Bain, a rooftop lounge with stunning city views and a lively dance floor.


Day 2: Iconic Landmarks and Museums

  • 8:00 AM - Breakfast: Start the day with a classic bagel and lox at Russ & Daughters, an iconic Lower East Side institution.

  • 9:30 AM - Morning: Visit the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island (make sure to book tickets in advance).

  • 12:00 PM - Lunch: Grab a hot and delicious pastrami sandwich at Katz's Delicatessen, a famous spot loved by locals and visitors alike.

  • 2:00 PM - Afternoon: Explore the National September 11 Memorial & Museum.

  • 7:30 PM - Dinner: Dine in style at the elegant and historic Gramercy Tavern, known for its seasonal American cuisine.

  • 10:00 PM - Nightlife: Catch a Broadway show or musical performance at one of the iconic theaters. After the show, head to Bar Centrale for a speakeasy-style cocktail experience in the heart of the theater district.


Day 3: Cultural and Artistic Experience

  • 9:00 AM - Breakfast: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at Bubby's, known for its mouthwatering pancakes and comfort food.

  • 10:30 AM - Morning: Visit The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met) and admire its vast collection.

  • 1:00 PM - Lunch: Try the delicious falafel sandwich at Mamoun's Falafel, a popular and affordable spot in Greenwich Village.

  • 3:00 PM - Afternoon: Explore the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum or another art gallery of choice.

  • 7:00 PM - Dinner: Indulge in classic New York-style pizza at Lombardi's, America's first pizzeria.

  • 9:30 PM - Nightlife: Experience the vibrant nightlife of the East Village, known for its diverse bars and live music venues. Check out Rockwood Music Hall for a range of live performances and a lively atmosphere.


Day 4: Central Park and Shopping

  • 8:30 AM - Breakfast: Start the day with a delightful breakfast at Norma's, known for its extravagant breakfast creations.

  • 10:00 AM - Morning: Enjoy a leisurely walk or bike ride in Central Park.

  • 12:00 PM - Lunch: Grab a hot dog from the iconic Nathan's Famous in Coney Island, a true New York experience.

  • 2:00 PM - Afternoon: Shop along Fifth Avenue and visit iconic stores like Tiffany & Co. and Saks Fifth Avenue.

  • 7:30 PM - Dinner: Dine at Per Se, an upscale restaurant with breathtaking views and the chance to spot celebrities.

  • 10:00 PM - Nightlife: Experience the bustling nightlife of Times Square with its theaters and entertainment. Visit Times Square's iconic Irish pub, O'Lunney's Times Square Pub, for live music and a lively pub atmosphere.


Day 5: Neighborhood Exploration

  • 9:00 AM - Breakfast: Enjoy a classic New York breakfast at The Smith, a trendy restaurant with a diverse menu.

  • 10:30 AM - Morning: Visit the famous Rockefeller Center and Top of the Rock Observation Deck.

  • 1:00 PM - Lunch: Try the famous Reuben sandwich at Katz's Delicatessen for a satisfying midday meal.

  • 3:00 PM - Afternoon: Explore the trendy neighborhoods of SoHo and Greenwich Village.

  • 7:30 PM - Dinner: Experience the sophisticated ambiance and delectable French cuisine at Daniel, a high-end culinary destination.

  • 10:00 PM - Nightlife: Discover the vibrant nightlife in the West Village with its live music venues and bars. Head to Smalls Jazz Club for an intimate and authentic jazz experience.


Day 6: Museum and Cultural Delights

  • 9:00 AM - Breakfast: Start the day with a delicious bagel and cream cheese from Ess-a-Bagel, a beloved bagel spot.

  • 10:30 AM - Morning: Visit the American Museum of Natural History.

  • 1:00 PM - Lunch: Grab a hot dog from Gray's Papaya, known for its affordable and tasty offerings.

  • 3:00 PM - Afternoon: Explore the vibrant Chinatown and Little Italy neighborhoods.

  • 7:00 PM - Dinner: Dine at The Grill at The Four Seasons, a restaurant frequented by celebrities and offering a chic dining experience.

  • 10:30 PM - Nightlife: Experience the lively nightlife in the Lower East Side with its diverse bars and music venues. Head to Pianos for live music, DJs, and a vibrant dance floor.


Day 7: Personal Exploring and Departure

  • 9:30 AM - Breakfast: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at Clinton St. Baking Company, famous for its pancakes and brunch options.

  • 11:00 AM - Morning: Free exploring time - visit a spot of interest you haven't seen yet or simply relax.

  • 1:00 PM - Lunch: Try the mouthwatering chicken and rice from The Halal Guys, a beloved street food vendor.

  • 3:00 PM - Afternoon: Free exploring time - indulge in shopping or another favorite activity.

  • 7:30 PM - Dinner: For the final night, indulge in a memorable dinner at Eleven Madison Park, a three-Michelin-star restaurant known for its exceptional culinary creations.

  • 10:00 PM - Nightlife: Enjoy a leisurely evening stroll through the beautiful High Line park, followed by a visit to the rooftop bar at The Standard, High Line, for breathtaking views of the city.

Rome Itinerary

Day 1: Exploring Ancient Rome

  • 8:30 AM: Depart from the hotel.

  • 9:00 AM: Visit the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill. Explore the ancient ruins and learn about their fascinating history.

  • 12:30 PM: Lunch at "Hostaria da Nerone," a charming restaurant near the Colosseum, serving traditional Roman dishes.

  • 2:00 PM: Explore the Capitoline Hill and its museums, offering breathtaking views of the city.

  • 5:00 PM: Return to the hotel for some relaxation and freshening up.

  • 7:30 PM: Dinner at "La Carbonara," a popular trattoria, known for its authentic carbonara pasta.

  • 9:30 PM: Optional evening walk around the Trevi Fountain to see it beautifully lit up at night.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 2: Vatican City and Pantheon

  • 8:00 AM: Depart from the hotel.

  • 9:00 AM: Visit Vatican City, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Vatican Museums.

  • 12:30 PM: Lunch at "Pizzarium," a renowned pizzeria near the Vatican, famous for its mouthwatering pizza by the slice.

  • 2:00 PM: Explore the Pantheon and enjoy gelato at "Giolitti," a historic gelateria nearby.

  • 5:00 PM: Return to the hotel for some rest and relaxation.

  • 7:30 PM: Dinner at "Ristorante Maccheroni," a charming restaurant near Piazza Navona, serving traditional Roman cuisine.

  • 9:30 PM: Optional evening stroll through the vibrant streets of Trastevere, with its lively bars and entertainment.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 3: Borghese Gallery and Appian Way

  • 8:00 AM: Depart from the hotel.

  • 9:00 AM: Visit the Borghese Gallery and its stunning art collection (pre-booked tickets).

  • 12:30 PM: Lunch at the Gallery's café.

  • 2:00 PM: Explore the Appian Way and visit the Catacombs (pre-booked tickets).

  • 5:00 PM: Return to the hotel for some leisure time.

  • 7:30 PM: Dinner at "Ristorante Piperno," a historic Jewish-Roman trattoria in the Jewish Ghetto, known for its Roman-Jewish cuisine.

  • 9:30 PM: Optional evening walk along the Tiber River.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 4: Renaissance and Baroque Rome

  • 8:30 AM: Depart from the hotel.

  • 9:00 AM: Visit the Spanish Steps, Piazza di Spagna, and the Trevi Fountain.

  • 12:30 PM: Lunch at "Da Francesco," a family-run restaurant near the Pantheon, serving delicious Roman dishes.

  • 2:00 PM: Explore the Piazza Navona and its stunning Baroque fountains.

  • 5:00 PM: Return to the hotel for some free time.

  • 7:30 PM: Dinner at "Armando al Pantheon," a delightful restaurant near the Pantheon, known for its classic Roman recipes.

  • 9:30 PM: Optional night walk near the Colosseum.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 5: Farewell and Departure

  • 8:30 AM: Depart from the hotel.

  • 9:00 AM: Visit the Altare della Patria (Monumento Nazionale a Vittorio Emanuele II) and enjoy panoramic views of Rome.

  • 11:00 AM: Explore the bustling market at Campo de' Fiori.

  • 1:00 PM: Farewell lunch at "Ristorante Armando al Pantheon" for a memorable final meal together.

  • 3:00 PM: Return to the hotel for check-out and departure.

I hope you all have a fantastic time exploring Rome's rich history, culture, and cuisine. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to ask me anytime. Let's make unforgettable memories on this journey through the Eternal City!

Itinerary: seven-day bus tour of Greece

Day 1: Arrival in Athens

  • 9:00 AM: Arrive in Athens and check-in at the hotel.

  • Breakfast: Enjoy a delightful Greek breakfast at the hotel, with yogurt, honey, fruits, and pastries.

  • 11:00 AM: Guided tour of the Acropolis, including the Parthenon and other historical sites.

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at "Dionysos Zonar's," a restaurant with a view of the Acropolis, offering traditional Greek dishes with a modern twist.

  • 3:00 PM: Visit the Acropolis Museum to learn more about the artifacts and history.

  • 6:00 PM: Return to the hotel for some rest and relaxation.

  • 8:00 PM: Welcome dinner at "Taverna tou Psirri," a traditional Greek taverna with a warm atmosphere, serving classic Greek mezes and grilled specialties.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 2: Discovering Delphi

  • 8:00 AM: Depart from Athens for Delphi.

  • Breakfast: "Katoi Arachovas," a family-run café in the heart of Arachova, with aromatic Greek Coffee and homemade "bougatsa" filled with sweet custard or savory cheese, a popular Greek breakfast pastry.

  • 11:00 AM: Explore the ancient site of Delphi, home of the Oracle of Apollo.

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at "Kastalia Boutique Hotel Restaurant," a charming eatery with views of the Delphi ruins, offering authentic Greek cuisine.

  • 3:00 PM: Visit the Delphi Archaeological Museum.

  • 6:00 PM: Travel to Kalambaka.

  • 8:00 PM: Dinner at "Meteora Restaurant," a cozy restaurant in Kalambaka, known for its hearty Greek stews and grilled meats.

  • 10:00 PM: Check-in at the hotel.

Day 3: Meteora Monasteries

  • 8:00 AM: Visit the breathtaking Meteora monasteries perched on cliffs.

  • Breakfast: Enjoy a buffet breakfast at the hotel, featuring Greek yogurt, fresh fruits, and traditional pastries.

  • 11:00 AM: Depart for Thessaloniki.

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at "Ta Karamanlidika Tou Fani," a historic delicatessen in Thessaloniki, offering an array of local Greek delicacies.

  • 3:00 PM: Explore the White Tower and the Archaeological Museum of Thessaloniki.

  • 6:00 PM: Return to the hotel for some leisure time.

  • 8:00 PM: Dinner at "Ergon Agora," a vibrant food market with various food stations, allowing you to try different Greek specialties.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 4: Exploring Thessaloniki

  • 8:00 AM: Visit the Rotunda and Arch of Galerius.

  • Breakfast: "Theopetra Estate," a charming cafe and farm that offers a farm-to-table breakfast experience. Savor organic eggs, freshly squeezed juices, artisanal cheeses, and homemade preserves, all sourced from their own farm and local producers, providing a genuine taste of the region's flavors.

  • 11:00 AM: Depart for Vergina.

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at "Kritikos," a family-run taverna in Vergina, offering homemade Greek dishes with a focus on local ingredients.

  • 3:00 PM: Explore the Royal Tombs of Vergina.

  • 6:00 PM: Return to Thessaloniki for some free time.

  • 8:00 PM: Dinner at "Nea Folia," a charming restaurant in the Ladadika district, serving innovative Greek dishes with a modern twist.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.



Day 5: Olympia and Nafplio

  • 8:00 AM: Depart from Thessaloniki for Olympia.

  • Breakfast: Enjoy a Greek breakfast at a local café in Thessaloniki, trying some local pastries like "bougatsa" or "koulouri."

  • 11:00 AM: Explore the ancient site of Olympia.

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at "Korê" in Olympia, offering fresh Mediterranean dishes with a focus on local produce.

  • 3:00 PM: Visit the Archaeological Museum of Olympia.

  • 6:00 PM: Travel to Nafplio.

  • 8:00 PM: Dinner at "Savouras," a traditional taverna in Nafplio, serving classic Greek dishes in a cozy setting.

  • 10:00 PM: Check-in at the hotel.

Day 6: Discover Nafplio and Epidaurus

  • 8:00 AM: Visit the Palamidi Fortress and enjoy stunning views of Nafplio.

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at a local café in Nafplio, with Greek coffee and "loukoumades" (Greek donuts).

  • 11:00 AM: Depart for the ancient theater of Epidaurus.

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at "To Karafaki," a family-run restaurant in Epidaurus, offering homemade Greek dishes and seafood.

  • 3:00 PM: Explore the ancient theater of Epidaurus and its impressive acoustics.

  • 6:00 PM: Return to Athens.

  • 8:00 PM: Farewell dinner at "Psarras," a renowned seafood restaurant in Athens, serving the freshest catches from the Aegean Sea.

  • 10:00 PM: Return to the hotel.

Day 7: Farewell and Departure

  • 9:00 AM: Breakfast at a local bakery or café in Athens, enjoying some "koulouri" or "spanakopita" (spinach pie).

  • 10:00 AM: Check-out from the hotel and departure from Athens.

  • Breakfast and lunch on this day will be at your leisure during your travels.

Sports Tourism


Event-based tourism: Sports event tourism involves traveling to another city or country to watch a sporting event. Examples of sports tourism in this category include traveling to watch major sporting events like the Olympics, the Super Bowl, or the World Cup.


Active sport tourism: This form of sports tourism revolves around actually participating in a sport while visiting a destination. Some travelers choose tourism destinations known for a specific sport or outdoor activity. Examples of active sports tourism include surfing in Hawaii or skiing in Colorado. Some tours include teams or sports clubs who play in live sports matches.



Active Team Sports Itinerary

by https://www.sportatours.com/


All Sports tours are tailored to the individual requirements of the schools and clubs we work with and also vary according to sport. The following example cricket tour itinerary however, provides an idea of what a tour could feature:


Day 1 – Arrive at London Heathrow at breakfast time. A representative from Sporta Tours will be in the arrivals hall to meet and assist you. From here, transfer to the Natural History Museum for a three-hour tour by coach and on foot to take in many of London’s top sights, including the Houses of Parliament, the London Eye, St. Paul’s Cathedral and Tower Bridge.


Depart for Taunton in the south-west of England after lunch and check-in to a hotel for two nights’ bed and breakfast accommodation. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure with a trip into Taunton, or rest and recover at the hotel before dinner.


Day 2 – After breakfast spend time sightseeing, or you may prefer to head over to Taunton School to make use of their sporting facilities prior to your first match of the tour in the afternoon. Enjoy post-match refreshments with your hosts before returning to the hotel for the evening.


Day 3 – After breakfast check-out and transfer to Gloucestershire. On arrival, check-in to the Royal Hop Pole Hotel in Tewkesbury, a well-known landmark and historic hotel, and enjoy lunch before meeting your next opponents at Tewkesbury School. Spend the evening in your new location.


Day 4 – Breakfast in the hotel before departing for Stratford. Enjoy a guided walking tour of the town, then continue on to Gloucester. Play your third match against King’s School Gloucester followed by post-match refreshments with your hosts. Return to your hotel for the evening.


Day 5 – After breakfast check-out of the hotel and travel north to Alton Towers where you will enjoy a full day at Britain’s biggest theme park. Then check-in to the Alton Towers Hotel at the theme park for your overnight stay. This unique hotel is themed around the incredible explorer Sir Algenon, who has made Alton Towers Hotel his home. There are plenty of fun and quirky features to discover, including his amazing flying ship which crash-landed in the middle of the hotel.


Day 6 – After breakfast check-out and it’s off to Derby through the picturesque countryside of the Peak District. Alternatively, head straight to Repton School for the next fixture. After the game, continue on to Nottingham, well known for its tales of Robin Hood and Sherwood Forest. Stay here for a night within a local hotel.


Day 7 – After breakfast enjoy some free time before your next match against Trent College in Nottingham. In the evening, enjoy a tour prize-giving ceremony before departing the next day.


Day 8 – After breakfast check-out of the hotel and transfer to London for a tour of Lord’s cricket ground. Following the tour, continue to Heathrow Airport for your return flight.
