Cuffed Couple

  1. Marissa and Gary were having relationship troubles but they didn’t want to break up.
  2. They thought by handcuffing themselves together, it would force them to face their issues.
  3. Marissa wanted to look at their relationship from a different vantage point.
  4. Gary was fatigued from arguing all the time.
  5. Some of the biggest obstacles were things like getting dressed and brushing their teeth.
  6. The real problems came when they wanted to do chores like washing dishes and mopping.
  7. Because they didn’t do things the same way, they ended up fighting more.
  8. They soon realized that they weren’t meant for each other. They broke up.
