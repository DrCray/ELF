Best for Success

Self learning: The most successful people put reading first. They are self-taught.

Prioritize: Focus your time on things that will actually further your success.

Curiosity: The first step of creativity is having a curious mind.

Be proactive: Just do it. Don’t wait for others. Don’t let others negatively influence you.

Be positive: Things that seem like failures are just steps that lead to more success.

Teamwork: You can’t do everything by yourself. Don’t micro-manage others.

Help others: It helps with business ideas. Volunteer for charity work. Get involved.
