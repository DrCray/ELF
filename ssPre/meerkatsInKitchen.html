Meerkats in the kitchen



Getting Started

One sunny afternoon, two meerkats named Martin and Shanna sneaked into a house through an open window. The delicious smell of freshly cooked food led them straight to the kitchen. Their tiny stomachs were growling, as they hadn't eaten since breakfast.

The hungry meerkats began exploring the kitchen. Curious, Shanna opened a bottom cabinet door. Unfortunately, a frying pan fell out with a loud clang, landing right on Martin's head. He rubbed his head and and complained to Shanna. 

While Martin was still complaining, Shanna skillfully climbed up the cabinet door and onto the kitchen counter. Martin followed her, still rubbing the bump on his head.

On the counter, Shanna spotted a wooden cutting board leaning against the wall. In her excitement to look behind it for food, she knocked it over. The heavy board landed with a thump right on Martin's foot. Poor Martin yelped, hopping around on one foot while holding on to the other. 

When he finally settled down, he found himself standing on something strange – the handle of a turned up spatula. Seeing an opportunity for mischief, Shanna ran across the counter and leaped onto the flat end of the spatula. Poor Martin went flying through the air like a furry rocket and landed with a splat in a colander that was sitting on the stove top.

In his attempt to get out of the strainer, Martin grabbed the nearest thing he could reach – the handle of a large pot. His plan backfired when the pot flipped over, trapping Martin inside. Desperate to free himself, Martin started pushing and bumping around the inside of the pot. Realizing he could make the pot move, he began to slide the pot across the counter. But he pushed one too many times, and suddenly both he and the pot went tumbling off the countertop and down onto an open oven door below.

Shanna was laughing so hard at Martin's bad luck that she stumbled backward, bumping into a tall blender. Her paw hit one of the buttons, and the sudden loud noise made Sarah jump in surprise. She jumped so high and far that she landed on the oven door next to Martin.

After catching their breath, they began to laugh at themselves. Then they leaped from the oven door over to the kitchen island countertop. There, they found a big bowl of rice sitting next to a rice cooker. They ate until they were full and happy, then sat down next to the bowl to plan their next adventure.

Comprehension Questions
  1. Once inside the house, why did Martin and Shanna go into the kitchen?
  2. When Shanna opened the cabinet door, what hit Martin on the head?
  3. What landed on Martin's foot?
  4. When Martin found himself standing on a spatula, what did Shanna do?
  5. How did Martin end up in the colander?
  6. How did Martin end up under the pot?
  7. Martin pushed the pot on too many times and ended up where?
  8. How did the blender get turned on? Explain.
  9. Where did Shanna and Martin find the bowl of rice?
Discussion
  1. Try to name every item or appliance that can be found in a kitchen?
  2. Talk about what kinds of things you know how to cook.
