Unit 15
Car Thieves, K9s, and Gators
[based on actual events]
Getting Started
  • Do you know anyone whose car was stolen or broken into?
  • Have you ever been chased by an angry dog?
  • Have you ever seen a live gator or croc?

Car theft has become a common problem at Blinky's Casino in Florida. So the security guards and casino guests are always on high alert. They actually have a large sign at the parking lot entrance that reads:

Anyone spotting a suspected car thief, call our security line at 555-8888 immediately.

On one particular night at Blinky's, someone spotted two men attempting to break into a car and quickly called the security line. When the guards ran to investigate, they discovered two men breaking into a parked vehicle. The men tried to run, but the guard's K9 companion chased down and tackled one of the thieves who was so frightened by the dog that he began to cry.

The other criminal was even more unfortunate. He jumped into a small lake behind the casino and tried to out-swim the guards. But the guards weren't about to give chase because they heeded the warning of a very big sign that said: Beware of Gators!

It seems that this lake is home to a lot of alligators and that one nine-foot gator in particular was quite famous. The guests and the staff at the casino even had a name for it, Groucho.

When the bystanders saw Groucho swimming toward the thief, they began screaming at him to swim back to shore. The criminal, who probably thought the warnings were just a ruse to get him caught by the guards, kept on swimming. Eventually, everyone heard the thief scream just before he was dragged under water by the gator.

Divers later found the body 50 feet below the surface where the gator had lodged the body under a log for a later snack, as gators often do with their kill.

Game and Fish officers later tracked down the nine-foot gator and killed it. “We have a zero tolerance policy towards any gator that attacks a human. We find them and put them down as fast as possible.”

“Maybe we should come up with a new slogan to warn criminals,” said a casino guest, “that if the K9 cops don't get you, the gators will.”

Comprehension Questions
  1. What kind of problem was Blinky's casino having?
  2. What did a guest spot two men attempting to do? What did the guest do?
  3. When the security guards showed up, what did the criminals do?
  4. What did the guard's K9 companion do? What did the criminal do when?
  5. How did the other car thief attempt to escape from the guards?
  6. Why didn't the guards jump in the lake after the car thief?
  7. When bystanders yelled at the thief to turn back, what did the thief probably think?
  8. What finally happened to the car thief? 
  9. In the end, what happened to Groucho, the nine-foot gator? Why?
  10. According to one guest, what should the casino's new slogan be?
Discussion
  1. You are walking down an alley, suddenly, you are being chased by a pack of dogs. What do you do?
  2. Have you ever had any unusual pets like bugs, birds, gators, snakes, or spiders? If no, what do you think would make an interesting and unsuual pet? Why?
  3. Is Florida's zero-tolerance policy for gators a fair policy? Why or Why not?
