Witch Switch

  1. Tim and Susie's mother mysteriously disappeared.
  2. The search party looked everywhere but never found her.
  3. The father remarried, but the woman didn't like children.
  4. One day the stepmother took the children out into the woods and left them there.
  5. She told their father that wild beasts had killed them.
  6. The children found a house of an ugly, but kind, old woman.
  7. The old woman sang a song to help them sleep and the kids recognized the song.
  8. The old woman suddenly became young and beautiful and they could see it was their mother.
  9. The mother said the old witch had stolen her beauty and married their father.
  10. The old witch knew the spell was broken and hurried to the house in the woods.
  11. But the father had followed her, realized what had happened, knocked out the witch, and rescued his family.
