Teen in Retirement

  1. A friend told Talia about an apartment that was inexpensive and spacious.
  2. It was fully furnished and had all the necessary kitchen appliances.
  3. All residents at the complex have access to a swimming pool and badminton area.
  4. There is also a sauna, a hot tub, a jet tub, and a fitness gym.
  5. She rented the apartment sight unseen, because she was attending college nearby.
  6. She met a lot of elderly people and then realized she lived in a retirement community.
  7. The reason the apartment was cheap was because it is subsidized by the government.
  8. Even though it is subsidized, only eighty percent of residents have to be elderly.
  9. She thinks it's a fantastic experience living there.
