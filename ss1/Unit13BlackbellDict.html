Blackbell

  2. Agatha thinks it's her husband's fault that Blackbell doesn't like him.
  3. The husband was tired of being blamed for nothing, so he started kicking the cat.
  4. One day the cat attacked the husband who accidentally choked the cat unconscious.
  5. He gave the cat mouth-to-mouth, it woke-up startled, so it scratched his face.
  6. He threw the cat against the wall, knocking it unconscious again.
  7. It had fallen into the sink and was all wet, so he decided to dry it off.
  8. It woke up again, but was rather calm this time.
  9. Agatha came home and saw him gently rubbing the cat. She thought they had made-up.
  10. The man told a wild story about how a dog chased the cat into the rose bushes,
  11. and how he saved the cat and got scratches on his face.
