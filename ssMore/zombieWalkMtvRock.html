The Zombie Walk

About the Song:

A person encounters a "Zombie Walk", not realizing what it is. Though first afraid, the stranger decides to join the fun. The walk ends with a Zombie dance off. Then the "zombies" go pub crawling and talk about their favorite horror movies and shows (The Shining, Halloween, and Walking Dead).

Twilight falls across the town... shadows start to move around...

Verse 1

At the edge of dusk, the sky turns black,

I turned the corner and my pulse attacked.

Twisted figures drifting slow and strange,

Gray skin glowing in the streetlight range.

Bruised and ragged, closing in the night,

I thought I'd run, but they blocked the light.

Left and right, front and back, no way out,

Surrounded tight, fear rising like a shout.

Pre-Chorus

Heart pounding hard, I hit the ground,

Covered my head, no escape sound...

Chorus

Zombie walks appear from nowhere fast,

Give you a scare that won't last.

But they're dancing, parading through the streets,

Raising money, spreading good beats.

Zombies laughing at the bars all night,

Friendliest crowd under festival light.

Think the dead can't move? Watch them rise —

Grooving strong, full of surprise!

Verse 2

Then laughter broke through the eerie air,

A hand reached down: "Stand up, we're there."

No fright intended, just join the line,

This is our walk, it's party time.

Hours of makeup to look our worst,

Comfy shoes on for the long burst.

We shuffled miles, scared kids by chance,

But smiles followed in the festival dance.

Bridge

At the end spot, the beat drops low,

Zombies walk slow but steal the show.

Awkward steps turn sharp and bold,

Fast then slow in the story told.

We crawl five pubs, talk horror deep,

The Shining wins, Halloween's keep,

Walking Dead binge, endless night —

Undead vibes feel so right.
